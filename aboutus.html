ABOUT US

Welcome to our Website

The Antonian Computer Fraternity, established in 2008 at St.Anthony's College Kandy, is a dedicated community for students passionate about technology and computer science.
Our fraternity aims to foster a collaborative environment where members can enhance their skills, share knowledge, and innovate together.
We organize workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures to provide hands-on experiences and insights from industry experts.
Whether you're a beginner or an advanced programmer, the Antonian Computer Fraternity offers valuable resources and networking opportunities to support your journey in the ever-evolving world of technology.
Join us and be part of a vibrant community that drives technological advancement and creativity!
